  • The Bookworm’s
    Guide to Movies

    An Article by Maninder Kaur

  • Introduction

    I love movies… a little bit too much. Every day, I’ll watch a movie, and normally finish it. Name any movie franchise, and I’ve probably watched all the movies multiple times. Yet, despite how much I love movies, book-based movies are my favorite. In this article I’ll be breaking down some of my 4 favorite book-based movies.

  • The Martian

    The Martian is my FAVORITE movie. Not only is there Matt Damon (Already a 10/10), but the plot of the movie is amazing. There are not many book-based movies that are better to equal to their book counterparts, but The Martian has succeeded in that category too. A Must Watch…

  • American Psycho

    Another good movie based on a book is American Psycho. Look, the book was absolutely traumatizing, but the movie? Not so much (thankfully). Yet, every single time I watch it, I want to embrace the yuppie style, and try to look better. Cause you can always be thinner… look better.

  • The Shining

    Horror is a genre that is often hard to write about, yet is absolutely terrifying on screen. The Shining was surprisingly a very scary book. Maybe it was the amount of suspense that they built up. And the film? It is known as one of the most iconic film masterpieces ever.

  • Bladerunner

    Lastly, we have to give an ode to a dystopian film. The movie Bladerunner is a perfect dystopian film. It is based on the book Do Android’s Dream of Electric Sheep? I had to watch the movie twice, as the first time I was too busy looking at the graphics. A stunning movie with an even stunningier plot line.
